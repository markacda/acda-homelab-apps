Find the electricity price at which charging your car becomes cheaper than filling it with petrol.

crossover price (€/kWh)

How it's calculated

Petrol cost per km = petrol price ÷ consumption.
Electric energy per km = battery capacity ÷ range.
The two costs are equal when:

€/kWh = (petrol price × range) ÷ (consumption × capacity)

If your actual electricity tariff is below this number, charging is cheaper than petrol.
