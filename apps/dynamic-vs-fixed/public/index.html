        How it works & assumptions
        

          Each 15-minute interval of your usage is priced two ways: your fixed day/night tariff, and
          the matching historic EnergyZero market price built up to an all-in rate:
        

        
dynamic €/kWh = (market + markup + energy tax) × (1 + VAT)

        
    
          
  • 
            Gas is priced at the EnergyZero daily rate (gas isn't metered per 15 min).
          
    • 
          
  • Solar feed-in (teruglevering) is ignored — cost is on grid import only.
    • 
          
  • 
            Fixed monthly fees and the energy-tax rebate are equal for both contracts, so they
            cancel out.
          
    • 
          
  • If your export covers less than a year, results are scaled to a full year.
